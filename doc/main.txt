Who Turned Out The Lights?
--------------------------

A game about a kid on a quest to turn the lights back on.
It's dark and scary in the house, and it's a maze just to walk around.

Main Character:
    Terry Lumenfeld
    A kid who just wants the lights back on.

Main Enemy:
    Lumbers
    They're like shadows, but not. They want to keep the lights off.
    Terry beats them with a pipe wrench.
